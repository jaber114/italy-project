italian food

the popular food in italy

pizza



In America pizza usually falls into two categories: thick and cheesy Chicago style or thin and more traditional New York pizza. In Italy pizza also falls into two distinct categories: Italian pizza and the rest of the world. It might seem silly considering the basic ingredients, but one taste of a true Italian pizza and that’s it. You will never feel the same about this simple and delicious food again.


passta


Pasta consists of flour, water, salt and sometimes egg (for egg noodles). Over the centuries Italians invented a myriad of shapes and sauces to enhance the basically bland taste of pasta. Pasta in Italy is always served in small portions (90 grams uncooked) after the first appetizer.


gellato

THE ITALIAN WORD CONGELATO MEANS FROZEN. ... Although gelato is the Italian version of ice cream, it's not merely Dreyer's with a European, artisanal flair. Like ice cream, gelato contains milk, sugar, and flavorings such as fruit or nuts, but it has less cream than ice cream and usually no egg yolks.
